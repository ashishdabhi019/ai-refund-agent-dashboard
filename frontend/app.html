Refund Policy — ShopEase v2.1
When you can get a refund
Your item arrived damaged (photo proof needed)
You received the wrong item by mistake
Your item stopped working within 30 days of purchase
Your order hasn't arrived within 14 days of the expected date
When a refund isn't possible
Request made after 30 days (or 45 days for Gold & Platinum members)
Digital or downloadable products are not refundable
Sale and clearance items are final — no returns accepted
Items that have been used, washed, worn, or altered
You've already had a refund in the past 90 days
Orders still in transit — refunds can only be processed after delivery
Good to know
Gold & Platinum members enjoy an extended 45-day return window
Refunds take 5–7 business days back to your original payment method
We refund the item price only — shipping costs are not included
Member tier perks don't apply to digital or clearance items
